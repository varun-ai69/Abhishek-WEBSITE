Smart Irrigation

Smart irrigation technology uses weather data or soil moisture data to determine the irrigation need of the landscape. These products maximize irrigation efficiency by reducing water waste while maintaining plant health and quality.

Soil Monitoring

Soil moisture sensors measure the amount of water in the soil, vital for agriculture, environmental monitoring, and scientific research. They use electrical resistance or capacitance to determine moisture levels.

Weather Forecast

Weather forecasting is crucial for agriculture, enabling farmers to make informed decisions about planting, irrigation, pest control, and harvesting, ultimately optimizing crop protection and maximizing yields.

Crop Monitoring Sensor

Crop monitoring involves tracking the health and growth of plants in agricultural fields. It helps identify potential problems or diseases early and take preventive measures.
